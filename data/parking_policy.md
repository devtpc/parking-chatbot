XYZ Company Parking Lot – Information and Rules

General Information

The XYZ Company operates a private parking lot for employees and authorized visitors.  
The parking lot contains **20 parking spaces** and is intended only for company-related use.

Parking is **free of charge** for employees.

The parking lot is **open 24 hours a day (0–24)**, including weekends and holidays.

The parking lot is located at:

**XYZ Company Parking Lot**  
Vecsés, Lincoln út 1.  
Hungary

Access and Directions

The parking lot is located near Budapest Ferenc Liszt International Airport, in the Vecsés industrial area.

You can reach the parking lot in the following ways:

From **Budapest via M4 motorway**:

1. Take the **M4 motorway** towards Vecsés / Airport.
2. Take the **Vecsés / Airport Industrial Area exit**.
3. Follow the signs toward **Lincoln út**.
4. Turn onto **Lincoln út**.
5. The XYZ Company parking entrance is located at **Lincoln út 1**.

From **Repülőtérre vezető út (Airport Road)**:

1. Drive toward **Vecsés industrial zone**.
2. Continue toward **Lincoln út**.
3. Turn onto **Lincoln út**.
4. The parking entrance is located at **Lincoln út 1**.

Parking Entrance Procedure

The parking lot is located inside a gated company area.

At the gate:

1. Stop at the security checkpoint.
2. Tell the security guard **your name**.
3. Show your **company ID card**.
4. If you have a reservation, the guard will verify it in the system.
5. After verification, you may enter the parking area.

Vehicle Restrictions

Only standard passenger vehicles are allowed in the parking lot.

The following restrictions apply:

- Maximum vehicle weight: **3.5 tons**
- **Buses are not allowed**
- **Large trucks are not allowed**
- Oversized vehicles may be refused entry by security

Parking Rules

- Parking is allowed **only in designated parking spaces**.
- Do not block entrances, emergency exits, or other vehicles.
- Follow all instructions from security personnel.
- The company is not responsible for damage caused by improper parking.

Parking Reservations

Employees may request parking reservations using the chatbot system.

To request a reservation, the following information is required:

- Employee name
- Car number (license plate)
- Reservation start time
- Reservation end time

Reservation requests must be **approved by an administrator** before becoming active.

When a reservation is approved, the system assigns a parking lot number and the employee will be informed.

Parking Availability

Parking availability depends on the requested reservation period.

Two reservations overlap if:

existing.start_time < requested.end_time  
AND  
existing.end_time > requested.start_time

A parking lot is considered free if **no overlapping approved reservation exists for that parking lot**.

If all parking lots are occupied for a given period, new reservations may be rejected by the administrator.

Support

If you experience problems accessing the parking lot or with your reservation, please contact the company administrator or security staff at the gate.
